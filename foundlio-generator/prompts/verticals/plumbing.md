# Plumbing Vertical Guide

Industry-specific guidance for generating plumbing company websites.

## Industry Overview

Plumbing companies provide water and drainage system services for residential and commercial properties. Key characteristics:

- **Emergency-critical**: Water damage can be catastrophic
- **24/7 demand**: Plumbing emergencies happen anytime
- **Diverse services**: From simple fixes to major repairs
- **Trust-based**: Working inside homes requires credibility

## Services to Generate

### Primary Services (always include)

1. **Drain Cleaning** (emergency: true)
   - Clogged drains, slow drains
   - Features: hydro jetting, camera inspection, all drain types

2. **Water Heater Repair** (emergency: true)
   - No hot water, leaks, strange noises
   - Features: tank/tankless, gas/electric, same-day

3. **Water Heater Installation**
   - New installations, replacements
   - Features: tank, tankless, hybrid, sizing

4. **Leak Detection & Repair** (emergency: true)
   - Hidden leaks, water damage prevention
   - Features: electronic detection, slab leaks, pipe repair

5. **Sewer Line Service**
   - Sewer repair, replacement, cleaning
   - Features: trenchless repair, camera inspection, root removal

### Secondary Services (include 2-3)

6. **Repiping**
   - Whole-house pipe replacement
   - Features: copper, PEX, polybutylene replacement

7. **Fixture Installation**
   - Faucets, toilets, sinks, showers
   - Features: all brands, upgrades, accessibility

8. **Emergency Plumbing** (emergency: true)
   - 24/7 availability
   - Features: fast response, water shutoff assistance

## Licenses & Certifications

### State Licenses (vary by state)
- **Arizona**: ROC license (plumbing specialty)
- **California**: CSLB C-36 (Plumbing)
- **Texas**: State plumbing license
- **Florida**: State certified/registered plumber

### Industry Certifications
- Master Plumber (state-issued)

## Regional Considerations

### Hot Climates (AZ, NV, TX)
- Hard water issues (mineral buildup)
- Water heater strain from high input temps
- Outdoor plumbing sun damage
- Desert soil = slab leak concerns

### Cold Climates (CO, MN, MI, Northeast)
- Frozen pipe prevention/repair
- Winterization services
- Thawing services
- Insulation recommendations

### Coastal Areas (FL, CA coast)
- Salt water corrosion concerns
- Hurricane/flood preparedness
- Sump pump services
- Elevated water heaters

### Older Cities (Northeast, Midwest)
- Aging infrastructure
- Lead pipe concerns
- Cast iron replacement
- Root intrusion common

## Local Challenges by Region

### Phoenix/Arizona
- Extremely hard water (mineral buildup)
- Slab foundations = slab leak concerns
- Desert soil expansion/contraction
- Polybutylene pipe issues (80s-90s homes)
- High water heater temperatures needed

### Dallas/Texas
- Clay soil movement affects pipes
- Hard water (varies by area)
- Older cast iron sewer lines
- Tree root intrusion common

### Denver/Colorado
- Frozen pipe season (Oct-April)
- Mountain communities = challenging access
- High altitude affects water pressure
- Snowmelt runoff = drainage issues

### Miami/Florida
- Hurricane/flood damage
- High water table
- Salt water intrusion
- Coral rock = difficult excavation
- Septic systems common in suburbs

### Los Angeles/California
- Earthquake pipe damage
- Aging infrastructure
- Water conservation requirements
- Slab-on-grade construction
- Drought restrictions
