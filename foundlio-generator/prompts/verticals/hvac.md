# HVAC Vertical Guide

Industry-specific guidance for generating HVAC company websites.

## Industry Overview

HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) companies provide climate control services for residential and commercial properties. Key characteristics:

- **Emergency-driven**: AC failures in hot climates are emergencies
- **Seasonal**: Peak demand in summer (cooling) and winter (heating)
- **Technical**: Requires licensed, certified technicians
- **Relationship-based**: Maintenance contracts create recurring revenue

## Services to Generate

### Primary Services (always include)

1. **AC Repair** (emergency: true)
   - Same-day service focus
   - All brands serviced
   - Features: diagnostic, refrigerant, compressor, electrical

2. **AC Installation**
   - New system installation
   - Replacement/upgrades
   - Features: sizing, efficiency ratings, brands, warranties

3. **AC Maintenance**
   - Preventive tune-ups
   - Seasonal preparation
   - Features: point inspection, cleaning, filter replacement

4. **Heating Repair** (emergency: true)
   - Furnace and heat pump repair
   - Features: gas, electric, heat pump, safety checks

5. **Heating Installation**
   - New furnace/heat pump
   - Features: efficiency, sizing, fuel options

### Secondary Services (include 2-3)

6. **Duct Cleaning**
   - Air quality improvement
   - Features: NADCA process, before/after photos

7. **Indoor Air Quality**
   - Air purifiers, UV lights, humidifiers
   - Features: filtration, humidity control, allergen reduction

8. **Emergency HVAC Service** (emergency: true)
   - 24/7 availability
   - Features: fast response, after-hours, no overtime charges

## Licenses & Certifications

### State Licenses (vary by state)
- **Arizona**: ROC (Registrar of Contractors) license
- **California**: CSLB C-20 (HVAC)
- **Texas**: TDLR HVAC license
- **Florida**: State certified/registered HVAC contractor

### Industry Certifications
- NATE Certified (North American Technician Excellence)
- EPA 608 Certified (refrigerant handling)
- Carrier Factory Authorized Dealer
- Trane Comfort Specialist
- Lennox Premier Dealer
- BBB A+ Rating

## Regional Considerations

### Hot Climates (AZ, NV, TX, FL)
- Emphasize AC repair speed (emergency)
- Mention extreme heat survival
- Dust/filter considerations
- Extended summer hours

### Cold Climates (CO, MN, MI)
- Emphasize heating reliability
- Mention winter preparation
- Frozen pipe prevention

### Humid Climates (FL, LA, TX coast)
- Emphasize humidity control
- Mold/mildew prevention
- Indoor air quality focus

## Local Challenges by Region

### Phoenix/Arizona
- Extreme summer heat (115°F+)
- Dust storms (haboobs) clog filters
- Monsoon humidity (July-Sept)
- Year-round AC usage
- Older homes with outdated systems

### Dallas/Texas
- Hot summers, cold winters (both needed)
- Hail damage to outdoor units
- High humidity
- Rapid growth = new construction

### Denver/Colorado
- High altitude considerations
- Cold winters, mild summers
- Dry air (humidifiers)
- Mountain communities = access issues

### Miami/Florida
- Extreme humidity
- Hurricane/storm season
- Salt air corrosion (coastal)
- Year-round cooling
- Mold concerns
